# AI Governance, Security & Privacy

Patterns and templates for governing AI in enterprise environments responsibly. This section covers the operational side of AI risk: the controls, processes, and artifacts that turn principles into something teams actually run.

Companion writeups will live on the [portfolio site](https://maplecheeze.github.io/Portfolio/) as the work matures.

## What this section will cover

- **Governance**: frameworks (NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001), policy as code for AI workloads, AI inventory and risk registers, vendor and tooling assessments
- **Security**: controls for LLM and AI service deployments, prompt injection and abuse mitigations, model and data supply-chain integrity, telemetry for AI workloads
- **Privacy**: privacy-by-design patterns for AI features, data minimization, redaction and retention, DPIA templates and workflows
- **Responsible AI**: operationalizing principles, transparency and explainability practices, human-in-the-loop checkpoints, incident response patterns specific to AI failures
